How to Use

At the current version, Erm...Actually! is only available on Gmail. To use this on the available platforms:
1.) Open Gmail
2.) Open any email
3.) Click the Extension icon (looks like a puzzle piece) next to your search bar
4.) Enable the button
5.) Wait for the AI to finish its response
6.) Your result will be at the bottom right of the screen.

About

Erm...Actually! is a Chrome extension that helps users fact-check text on screen, particularly politically targeted content, as these can lead to scams and the spread of misinformation that influence public opinion and democratic decision-making.

As people in the modern world grow busier, encountering and unknowingly accepting misinformation has become increasingly common across the digital landscape.

Our goal is to make it easier for busy people, like college students, to make well-informed decisions.
